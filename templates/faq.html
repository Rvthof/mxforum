Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

This site looks very different than any other forums?

True, we based this forum on sites like stackoverflow and uservoice. Were older forums are controlled by assigned moderators we really like the idea that the most prominent users, who gained reputation by contributing valuable content, can have certain privileges to keep this forum clean. In other words, this forum is run by you.

Reputation?

For basic use of our forum, you do not need any reputation, asking questions, answering questions and simply viewing questions can be done by everyone.

But if you want to help running the site, you’ll need reputation. The more reputation you gain by contributing, the more we allow you to change and adjust content.

Reputation can be gained if your questions or helpful answers are voted up by other members. For every upvote, you gain 10 reputation points. But if you ask off topic questions or answers than you might be voted down: this means you lose 2 reputation points. You can only earn 200 reputation points a day. Any vote for posts that are marked “community wiki” cannot result in gaining reputation.

These are the amounts of reputation you need to have certain options:
15 Vote up
15 Flag offensive
50 Leave comments
100 Vote down, (costs 1 rep)
250 Vote to close or reopen your questions, create new tags
500 Retag questions
750 Edit Community Wiki Questions
2000 Edit other people's posts
3000 Vote to close or reopen any questions
5000 Delete Comments
10000 Delete questions, answers and other management functions
After earning enough reputation you will become as influencial to the community as a moderator, and the community is run by the members instead of an assigned moderator, which is much more democratic!
