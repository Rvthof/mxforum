What is community integration?

For normal use of the community question and answer, the points are not necessary.
We have repeatedly stated, CNProg you to run and maintain. If you want to help us to operate CNProg, you need a certain level of points. points is a rough measure of the community to have confidence in your data. who do not have to pay points or directly to you, but you have access to other users through the support and trust, "earned" the.

For example, if you mention a very helpful question or made a very useful answer, you will be voted in favor of other users. Instead, you raised the issue of persona non grata, or misleading the user's answer, you may vote by other users. Each vote will help you generate 10 community points would be deducted for each negative vote you 2 points. Daily vote in favor of others, you can only have 200 of points, which is the upper limit.
